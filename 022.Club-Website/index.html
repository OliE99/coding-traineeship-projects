SEDGEFIELD CC

We have a very active junior section from under 9's through to under 19's. We have boys, girls and mixed teams playing competitive softball & hardball cricket. Sedgefield Cricket Club is an ECB 'Focus Club' and has successfully been awarded 'ECB Clubmark Accreditation' which is a Sport England Clubmark status to confirm that Sedgefield is a safe, effective and child-friendly club.
